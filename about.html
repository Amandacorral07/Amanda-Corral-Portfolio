About

Started the pandemic studying to be a future nurse, ending the pandemic a future software developer

I enjoy creating things that live on the world-wide net. My interest in web development started in 2021 as I was awaiting my results from nursing school. I decided to pick up some new skills during my free time. My husband suggested I should look into web development. I stumbled upon SheCodes, a Coding Bootcamp specifically created for women. I signed up for the next available free class where I was introduced to HTML and CSS. In an instant my interest was peaked and I was hooked on wanting to learn everything I could about web development!
